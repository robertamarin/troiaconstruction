Our Story

Built by founders with global craftsmanship

Angelos started in Limassol, Cyprus, where at his family company he built over 50 timber-frame homes—the first to bring this construction style to the island. He moved to the United States to build on his own path, where he met Robert Amarin, an experienced contractor in the mining industry. They began with smaller projects, grew each time, and founded Troia Construction.

Rooted in La Jolla, Serving Greater San Diego

Operating from Prospect Street in La Jolla, Troia Construction supports home owners, developers, and architects throughout the region with tailored project delivery. Our founders are on-site, on the phone, and accountable from strategy through closeout.

Process

Five steps from consultation to turnover

  1. Initial Consultation & Site Assessment – We review your property to determine the best use of space.
  2. Custom Design & Planning – Our designers draft ADU layouts that are comfortable and functional.
  3. Permitting & Approvals – We take care of all required documentation, local regulations, and required city permits.
  4. Construction & Project Management – Our ADU construction experts will build to your specifications using quality materials and attention to detail.
  5. Final Inspection & Turnover – We make sure your ADU meets all standards and is move-in ready.
