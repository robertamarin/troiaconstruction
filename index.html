Five steps from consultation to turnover

  1. Initial Consultation & Site Assessment – Evaluate property lines, access, and utilities to map the highest and best use for your ADU.
  2. Custom Design & Planning – Draft comfortable layouts, structural strategies, and MEP pathways tailored to your site.
  3. Permitting & Approvals – Coordinate documentation, jurisdictional reviews, coastal overlays, and neighbor notifications on your behalf.
  4. Construction & Project Management – Self-perform framing and concrete while leading vetted trades to deliver on schedule with craftsmanship.
  5. Final Inspection & Handover – Commission systems, document quality, and turn over a move-in ready dwelling with clear closeout reporting.
