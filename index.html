He moved to the United States to build on his own path, where he met Robert Amarin, an experienced contractor in the mining industry. They began with smaller projects, grew each time, and founded TROIA Construction.

From our home base on Prospect Street in La Jolla, we guide homeowners, developers, and architects across Greater San Diego with tailored project delivery. Robert and Angelos stay on site, on the phone, and accountable from the first consultation through turnover.

Five steps from consultation to turnover

  1. Initial Consultation & Site Assessment – Review project goals, site logistics, and jurisdictional requirements to align scope, budget, and timeline from the start.
  2. Custom Design & Planning – Coordinate architects, engineers, and trade partners to shape buildable drawings, schedules, and cost models.
  3. Permitting & Approvals – Prepare documentation, manage jurisdictional reviews, and secure approvals across coastal overlays and municipalities.
  4. Construction & Project Management – Self-perform structural trades while leading vetted partners to deliver on schedule with craftsmanship and safety.
  5. Final Inspection & Handover – Commission systems, document quality, and provide a thorough turnover package with warranties and maintenance guidance.
