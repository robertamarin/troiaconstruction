Our Services

Troia Construction offers end-to-end construction management services tailored to meet the needs of commercial, industrial, and residential clients. Our multidisciplinary teams bring decades of experience to each project.

Pre-Construction Planning

Feasibility studies, budgeting, and scheduling that set your project up for success from the start. We provide transparent cost modeling and risk mitigation strategies.

General Contracting

Comprehensive project oversight, subcontractor coordination, and quality control to ensure every build aligns with design intent and regulatory standards.

Design-Build Delivery

Integrated design and construction services that accelerate timelines, reduce costs, and produce cohesive solutions tailored to your operational needs.

Renovations & Retrofits

Modernizing existing structures with minimal downtime. We focus on sustainable upgrades that enhance functionality and performance.

Safety Management

Dedicated safety officers and comprehensive training programs keep every site compliant and every worker protected.

Facility Maintenance

Long-term maintenance plans, emergency repairs, and preventative services that extend the lifespan of your assets.
