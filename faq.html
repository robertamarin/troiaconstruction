Frequently asked questions

The details matter. Here's how Troia Construction navigates permitting, project delivery, and client communication across San Diego County.

Where do you work?

Our headquarters is at 243 Prospect Street in La Jolla, California. From there we serve San Diego's coastal and inland communities—including La Jolla, Del Mar, Encinitas, Carlsbad, and Downtown.

Do you only build ADUs?

ADUs are a core specialty, but we also deliver custom residences, multifamily improvements, and boutique commercial builds. We frequently partner with clients on targeted trade scopes such as framing, foundations, roofing, and siding.

How hands-on are the founders?

Robert and Angelos attend weekly site walks, lead subcontractor coordination, and handle key client updates personally. They remain involved from preconstruction through turnover.

Can you help before design is complete?

Yes. Early collaboration allows us to refine budgets, align structural systems, review utility requirements, and flag potential permitting hurdles before they slow the project.

How are inquiries handled?

Contact form submissions route directly to Robert and Angelos. Replace the Formspree ID on the contact page with your endpoint and each inquiry will arrive in your inbox for immediate response.

Do you work with outside design teams?

Absolutely. We collaborate with architects, interior designers, and engineers throughout California and can recommend partners suited to your project's scope and aesthetic.
