Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about working with Troia Construction? We've compiled answers to the topics we hear most often. Reach out if you need more details.

What types of projects do you specialize in?

We specialize in commercial, industrial, and high-end residential construction. Our portfolio includes office towers, healthcare facilities, mixed-use developments, and custom homes.

How do you ensure projects stay on schedule?

Detailed planning, weekly progress reviews, and real-time reporting tools keep stakeholders aligned. Our experienced superintendents proactively resolve issues before they impact timelines.

Do you offer sustainable building solutions?

Yes. We incorporate energy-efficient systems, recycled materials, and green certifications such as LEED into our projects whenever possible.

Can you work with my architect or designer?

Absolutely. We collaborate closely with architects, designers, and consultants to deliver cohesive results. We can also provide design-build services.

Where do you operate?

Troia Construction serves clients across the Northeast and Mid-Atlantic regions, with the ability to mobilize teams nationwide for select projects.
