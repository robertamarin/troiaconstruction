Accessory dwelling units crafted for coastal living

From garage conversions to multi-level additions, Troia Construction delivers ADUs that unlock livable space, boost property value, and open the door to potential rental income.

Garage Conversions

Upgrade existing structures

We retrofit garages with new foundations, insulation, and utility runs so the finished suite feels like a purpose-built residence.

  • Code-compliant structural reinforcement and waterproofing
  • Custom cabinetry, kitchenette layouts, and bath packages
  • Integrated heating, cooling, and sound attenuation
Attached ADUs

Seamless extensions

Expand your primary home with connected suites that mirror existing architecture while meeting updated energy and egress requirements.

  • Structural tie-ins engineered for seismic resilience
  • Envelope detailing to preserve comfort and efficiency
  • Shared mechanical strategies that simplify maintenance
Detached ADUs

Private stand-alone living

We deliver ground-up casitas and two-story cottages with curated outdoor spaces, private entries, and separate utility metering.

  • Site planning, grading, and drainage coordination
  • Prefabricated or custom-framed structural systems
  • Landscape integration for patios, decks, and privacy screens
Room Additions

Flexible interior expansions

Add bedrooms, suites, or hybrid workspaces that flow with your existing floor plan and maximize every square foot of the property.

  • Interior reconfigurations to optimize circulation
  • Structural modifications with minimal disruption
  • Finish selections tailored to your design language

Process

Our five-step ADU delivery

  1. Initial Consultation & Site Assessment – We review your property to determine the best use of space.
  2. Custom Design & Planning – Our designers draft ADU layouts that are comfortable and functional.
  3. Permitting & Approvals – We take care of all required documentation, local regulations, and required city permits.
  4. Construction & Project Management – Our ADU construction experts will build to your specifications using quality materials and attention to detail.
  5. Final Inspection & Turnover – We make sure your ADU meets all standards and is move-in ready.
