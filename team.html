Meet the Founders

Troia Construction is a San Diego-based partnership led by Robert Amarin and Angelos Stavrou. We are hands-on builders who guide every project from pre-construction strategy to the final handoff.

Robert Amarin

Managing Partner & Co-Founder

Robert directs client strategy, financial controls, and project delivery. With two decades in ground-up construction, he aligns budgets, schedules, and design intent while keeping communication clear and proactive.

Angelos Stavrou

Operations Director & Co-Founder

Angelos oversees field execution, trade partner coordination, and quality assurance. His site leadership keeps ADUs, estates, and commercial improvements moving efficiently without sacrificing craftsmanship.
